Prediction of Road Traffic Accidents

Road Traffic Accidents (RTAs) are a major cause of death globally. Every year, millions of individuals perish on the roads.

In this project, we built a machine learning model that predicts the probability of RTAs in different locations in US.
We hope that this project can contribute to the improvement of road safety in United States.
